— The movement —

ALMA is not just an app. It's a movement.

For those who believe our generation deserves better than what was built for it. For those who want to reclaim their intimacy.

An app doesn't change an era. A movement, perhaps, might. That's why ALMA is not just a product. It's a cultural attempt, carried by everyone who wants to repair what the digital has broken in how we relate to one another.

What we carry publicly

Beyond the application itself, ALMA owns a voice. A critical position — clear, sometimes uncomfortable. We believe silence is not neutral in the face of a system that destroys our capacity to love.

Here are the public commitments that structure our action.

01

Radical transparency

We will publish our real numbers quarterly: users, retention, couples formed, moderation incidents, judicial outcomes. Without embellishment. Without omission.

02

Judicial cooperation

No complacency with violent or abusive behavior. Systematic cooperation with French law enforcement and judiciary for any serious incident reported.

03

Refusal of advertising

No advertising in ALMA, ever, in any form. No commercial partners inside the product. Our economic independence is our moral independence.

04

No data resale

Your data will never be sold, shared, or monetized to third parties. European hosting. End-to-end encryption. Deletion in two clicks.

05

Owned public voice

We will take public positions on societal issues related to our field: harassment, violence, mental health, gender fracture, platform regulation. Even when it disturbs.

06

Independent ethics committee

An external committee composed of sociologists, lawyers, and mental health professionals will oversee our practices. With public right of review on our structural decisions.
