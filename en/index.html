— Our refusals —

What ALMA refuses, by design.

— No advertising —

Never. We serve no advertiser, not now, not later.

— No ranking —

No score, no public likes, no visible social metric.

— No anonymity —

Mandatory ID verification. Full cooperation with law enforcement in cases of abuse.

— No ghosting —

Required to close every conversation with dignity before receiving new ones.

— No manipulation —

No predatory freemium. You pay once, you have everything.

— No data leaks —

European hosting. End-to-end encryption. Your data belongs to you.
