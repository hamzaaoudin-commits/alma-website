— Our vision —

A relational infrastructure in service of connection, not extraction.

Six structural principles that define ALMA. Not marketing promises: irreversible technical choices.

Everything ALMA refuses is an architectural decision, not a commercial option. Here are our six founding refusals, and what they concretely mean.

01

No advertising, ever

We serve no advertiser — not now, not later, not ever. Our only revenue comes from paying users and from our Date Pack program. This economic independence guarantees that our algorithm works for you, not against you.

02

No ranking, ever

No visible score, no public likes, no social metric. You will never know how many people "like" you nor where you stand in an invisible hierarchy. This absence of ranking protects the self-esteem of every user.

03

No anonymity

Mandatory identity verification with official ID for every member, validated within 24 hours, then deleted. Systematic cooperation with law enforcement for serious offenses. This rigor protects everyone — and particularly women, who deserve a space where they know who they're talking to.

04

No ghosting

Required to close every conversation with dignity before receiving new ones. If the exchange doesn't lead anywhere, you must express it respectfully to the other person. This structural friction teaches finishing things, and breaks one of the most toxic poisons of contemporary dating.

05

No manipulation

No predatory freemium, no paid add-ons, no manufactured notifications to lure you back. You pay the subscription once, you have everything. Our business model is aligned with your interest: we make money when you find love, not when you remain trapped.

06

No data leaks

Hosting exclusively in the European Union. End-to-end encryption of conversations. No data shared with third parties, ever. Right to deletion activatable in two clicks. External independent DPO. Quarterly audits by an ethics committee. Your data belongs to you.

The daily ritual

Beyond these refusals, ALMA offers an experience structured around a deliberately limited daily ritual. This scarcity is our organizing principle.

Three profiles per day, no more

At the time you choose (between 6 PM and 10 PM), you receive three profiles selected for you. Three is enough to have choice, and few enough to give each person the attention they deserve. You have 24 hours to decide whether to engage in conversation with any of them.

Thirty minutes of conversation, synchronized

When two people agree to talk, they schedule a 30-minute window together. Not a thread of messages dragging on for three weeks. A real presence at a specific moment, like an appointment.

A video call the next day

If the textual conversation worked for both of you, ALMA automatically schedules a 20-minute video call for the following day. No more. If the call leads nowhere, you close politely. If it leads to something, you move to the next step.

A meeting in a partner venue

After the call, you can book a Date Pack at €19 in one of our partner cafés or restaurants in Paris. The staff is trained to discreetly watch over the safety of the meeting. Digital is now just a bridge. Real life takes over.

An economy in service of connection

Our business model is designed to disappear from your life once you have found someone. A subscription substantially cheaper than the premium tiers of dominant platforms. Without hidden fees.

The day you find someone on ALMA, you can activate a "couple pause mode": both accounts are frozen for 3 to 12 months, without payment. If the relationship lasts, that's our pride. If it doesn't, you can resume.

Our success is measured by those who leave us because they have found. That's all that sets us apart.
