— The ALMA experience —

The product. Designed for encounter, not captivity.

Five simple steps that transform a digital encounter into real connection. And that let you leave the moment you have found.

The user journey

Here is how a day in ALMA unfolds, from the moment you sign up to the meeting in real life.

01

Sign-up & verification

You download the app, create your profile, and verify your identity with an official document. Validation within 24 hours. Documents deleted afterward — only an anonymous hash is kept to prevent banned users from creating new accounts.

02

Values questionnaire

You answer 30 questions that explore what truly matters to you: relationship to family, work, spirituality, children, freedom. These answers feed the algorithm so it can suggest people with whom genuine compatibility is possible.

03

The daily ritual

Each day, at the time you choose (between 6 PM and 10 PM), you receive 3 profiles selected for you. No more, no less. You have 24 hours to decide whether to engage in conversation. If you let them pass, these profiles will not return.

04

The scheduled conversation

When two people agree to talk, they schedule a 30-minute window together. No open thread. A dedicated moment. An AI watches in real time for problematic behaviors without ever surveilling intimate content.

05

The call & real meeting

If the conversation works, ALMA schedules a 20-minute video call for the next day. And if the call works, you can book a Date Pack at €19 in one of our partner cafés or restaurants in Paris, trained to discreetly watch over your safety.

06

The couple pause mode

The day you become a couple, you both activate "pause mode" together: your accounts are frozen for 3 to 12 months, without payment. If the relationship lasts, it's our pride. If it doesn't, you resume where you left off.
